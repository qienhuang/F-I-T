# Relief Card — Depression & Addiction (FIT)
## One-Page Quick Reference

### Goal
Preserve reversibility and restart minimal evolution.

---

### Classify First
- LOCKED → Safety + presence only
- FRAGILE → Emptiness Window
- RESTARTABLE → Micro-restart

---

### Never Do
- Inject meaning
- Demand improvement
- Compare
- Overload options

---

### Emptiness Window
- No goals
- No evaluation
- No irreversible acts

---

### Micro-Restart (All Required)
- Low risk
- Uncertain outcome
- External impact
- Small & repeatable

---

### Watch Signals
+ Spontaneous action
+ Less stagnation
– Self-blame
– Withdrawal

---

### Safety
Any self-harm signal → escalate immediately.
